My name is Zechariah Ogungbile and I am a data analyst who loves using data to make a difference. I grew up in Ibadan, Nigeria and studied Political Science and International Relations at Osun State University, graduating with Second Class Honours (Upper Division).

My final year project on conflict management in Nigeria sparked my interest in data analytics, as I faced challenges with data collection and analysis. I learned the basics of data analytics through self-teaching, using YouTube and other apps, and completed my Bsc research with a B grade. My research was praised as one of the most analytical in the department.

After graduating, I worked as a PR Executive for Media Arkxess, where I used strategic communication and analytical thinking to increase client engagement during a time of change and crisis. I improved key metrics by 10% with innovative content strategies. I wanted to improve my data analytics skills further, so I joined a Data Analytics Bootcamp on Lighthall. I learned how to use analytics tools like Excel, SQL, Power BI, Tableau and Python to draw insights from data.

With this experience, I started my data analytics career with an internship at FJ Ajayi International LTD, where I helped optimize marketing strategies with over one million customer data points. I improved my technical skills in tools like Tableau, Power BI, and Excel.

Today, I am a Risk Analyst at Metropolitan Global Resources, where I lead risk assessment initiatives across various operations. I use advanced analytical techniques to enable better decision-making and a safer work environment. I also participate in research consortiums on urgent issues in the Niger Delta region and beyond as the Consortium Secretary (Zone B) of the GLGEF-AHEF Niger Delta Oil Spillage.

I have a proven record of applying multidisciplinary perspectives to real-world problems. I am ready to take my career to the next level. I am committed to continuous learning and passionate about creating positive impact through data.
