My Dashboards

The following visualizations projects are dashboards I created using Tableau, Power BI, and Excel. With the notable Key Points Indicators (KPI's) in the dashboards, I was able to draw insights and make recommendations where necessary to clients and stakeholders. A short explanation and link to view the dashboards has been attached for your perusal.

Sales Analysis and Visualization Using Excel

I used Excel to analyze sales data from an international superstore with branches in Nigeria and South Africa. I applied formulas, functions, and pivot tables to explore the data and find trends and patterns in KPIs (Key Performance Indicators). Based on my analysis, I suggested some insights and recommendations to boost the store's performance. You can view my project by clicking the button below.

KMS Superstore Sales Analysis

I created a Tableau dashboard to visualize the KMS superstore data. Sprocket Customer Analysis Dashboard

I used Tableau to create a dashboard on the Customer Analysis of Sprocket Central Pty Ltd, a bike-selling company. I explored the customer base, their demographics, and purchasing behavior. I suggested some observations and recommendations to the clients, such as targeting specific segments, giving discounts and incentives, and making customer profiles and personas.
